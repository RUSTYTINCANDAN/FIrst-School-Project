Daniel's Roadmap

A roadmap to guide me through high school and college

TO THE MAP!!!

Freshman Year

During my freshman year, I will focus on:

  • Taking a variety of classes to help me pursue my projects
  • Building a strong foundation in core subjects
  • Transitioning into a college high school
  • Working on projects I enjoy doing

Sophomore Year

In my sophomore year, I will:

  • Take more challenging courses
  • Explore potential career paths
  • Participate in volunteer work or community service
  • Research colleges and internships

Junior Year

During my junior year, I will focus on:

  • Taking more advanced courses and challenging myself academically
  • Preparing for standardized tests like the SAT or ACT
  • My projects and looking for ways to improve on them
  • Trying to create an app or website

Senior Year

In my senior year, I will:

  • Select colleges and submit applications
  • See which colleges best fit me
  • Start preparing my college essays
  • Maintain strong academics
